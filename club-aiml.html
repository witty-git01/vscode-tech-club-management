About the AI & ML Club

The Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Club is dedicated to exploring cutting-edge AI and ML concepts, including deep learning, computer vision, natural language processing, and more. The club organizes regular coding sessions, workshops, and AI model building competitions.

Students work on practical projects using Python, TensorFlow, and other modern libraries to gain hands-on exposure to real-world machine learning problems. The club fosters innovation, creativity, and collaboration to prepare students for the rapidly evolving AI industry.
