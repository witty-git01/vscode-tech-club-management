About the Cloud Computing Club

The Cloud Computing Club focuses on educating students about cloud platforms like AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud. Members learn how to deploy applications, manage cloud infrastructure, and explore trending topics such as containerization, microservices, and serverless computing.

Through workshops, certification prep sessions, and hands-on labs, students gain valuable exposure to the backbone technologies that power modern businesses. Whether you're new to cloud or looking to deepen your expertise, this club welcomes all technology enthusiasts.
