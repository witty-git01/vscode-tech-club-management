About the Web Development Club

The Web Development Club brings together students passionate about building modern, responsive, and interactive websites using the latest technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and popular frameworks. We conduct weekly sessions, hands-on workshops, and mini hackathons to strengthen practical coding skills.

In addition to web design fundamentals, the club focuses on real-world web app development, user experience (UX), performance optimization, and even a bit of backend with Node.js and APIs. No matter your skill level—beginner or advanced—you’ll find opportunities to learn, collaborate, and create.
