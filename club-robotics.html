About the Robotics Club

The Robotics Club brings together students interested in designing, building, and programming robots for various real-world applications. From line-following bots to autonomous vehicles, members learn to work with Arduino, Raspberry Pi, sensors, motors, and control systems.

Hands-on sessions, inter-college competitions, and collaborative projects allow students from diverse engineering backgrounds to develop problem-solving and technical skills that extend beyond textbooks. The club fosters creativity, teamwork, and innovation in the exciting field of robotics.
