About the Data Science Club

The Data Science Club helps students dive deep into the world of data analysis, machine learning, and artificial intelligence using Python, R, and cutting-edge tools. Members work on real-world datasets and build predictive models while learning essential statistics and visualization techniques.

The club focuses on both theoretical foundations and practical applications, including data wrangling, model building, and working with big data platforms. Members regularly take part in Kaggle competitions, hands-on coding sessions, and collaborative projects.
